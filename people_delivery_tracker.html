Recall & orphan quality issues
Non-basis recall issues (name tokenization, real property, DMV, Dockets) could fail NPD sign-off. Orphaned entities post-cutover create downstream application failures. No fallback if recall gap is material.

Privacy Delete Readiness — CRITICAL PATH
Non-basis privacy deletes are a legally obligated hard dependency. Confirmed as a Critical Path blocker. Must be complete and validated before cutover proceeds. Owner: RA / Assembly.
Medium
Historical relationship quality
Historical entity-to-entity relationships (basis record realignment) may degrade under TRPA vs ERD. Scenario 3 edge cases not fully tested. Could surface post-cutover in Westlaw PR and search.
Medium
Disaster recovery readiness
No confirmed DR plan for post-cutover failure scenario. Backup/standby strategy, restore/replay capability and post-rollback validation approach not yet documented. Must be in place before Go/No-Go gate. Criteria TBD.
Medium
Burn-In & Steady-State criteria undefined
No defined exit criteria for burn-in validation or formal definition of steady-state. Without these, there is no objective signal to declare cutover complete. Needs to be defined before cutover window.
